What is a "Nocab"?

The term “nocab” is just “bacon” spelled backwards. It’s pronounced No Cab (like if there weren't any taxis around). The word services a few purposes:

  • It’s my calling card. I use it to mark things that I’ve made like this website.
  • It's my go-to temporary variable name.
  • It acts a unique namespace for my hand-rolled tools. For example, Unity has its own "Message" functions but if I created my own object messaging infrastructure I may call it "NocabMessage" to differentiate.
